üåê Web Copilot

What It Knows

General knowledge and public internet information only. It does NOT know about your documents, emails, or Teams conversations.

Best For

  • Researching regulatory frameworks and legislation
  • Understanding industry trends and developments
  • Getting definitions and explanations
  • Brainstorming ideas for projects

Example Prompts:

"What are the key requirements of the Money Laundering Regulations 2017?"

"Explain common fraud typologies in simple terms"

‚úÖ Great for general research and learning
‚ùå Cannot access your files or internal systems

üìù Word Copilot

What It Knows

Only the specific Word document you currently have open. It can read and work with YOUR document content.

Best For

  • Drafting policy papers and guidance documents
  • Summarizing long documents
  • Rewriting sections for clarity or tone
  • Creating document outlines

Example Prompts:

"Summarize this 20-page document into 5 key themes"

"Make this section more concise and professional"

"Create an outline for guidance on fraud prevention"

‚úÖ Works with YOUR document content
‚ùå Only sees the current document - not your other files
