‚öñÔ∏è CPIA 1996 - Criminal Procedures and Investigations Act:

CRITICAL WARNING: Be extremely careful when using Copilot for live operations or any work involving criminal investigations, enforcement actions, or sensitive operational matters.

Why this matters: Under CPIA 1996, material generated or accessed during an investigation may be subject to disclosure requirements in criminal proceedings. This includes:

  • AI-generated content and prompts used in investigations
  • Analysis or summaries created by Copilot
  • Any material that might reasonably be considered relevant to the investigation

Safe practice: For live operations and enforcement work, avoid using Copilot entirely unless you are certain the material would not create disclosure obligations or compromise operational integrity.

When in doubt: Consult with your line manager or legal team before using Copilot for any enforcement-related work.

‚ö†Ô∏è Copilot Transcribe Rules ‚ñº

üîî REQUIREMENT: Inform all participants

You MUST tell all meeting participants at the start that you are using Copilot Transcribe.

How to say it: "Just to let everyone know, I'm using Copilot Transcribe for this meeting to help with note-taking."

‚è∞ 7-Day Auto-Delete

Copilot Transcribe automatically deletes transcripts after 7 days. If you need to retain information for legitimate business purposes, save key points to appropriate systems BEFORE the 7-day deadline.

Don't: Save entire transcripts unnecessarily - only extract what's needed and proportionate.

üö´ Do NOT use Transcribe for meetings containing:
